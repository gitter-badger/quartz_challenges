    Right on the Las Vegas Strip, this 5-star hotel and casino is adjacent to Fashion Show Mall. It offers luxury accommodations, 5 on-site restaurants, and an award-winning spa.

    The spacious suites at the Encore at Wynn Las Vegas feature modern décor with floor-to-ceiling windows. Each suite comes with at least 2 flat-screen TVs and a living room with a minibar. A work area with state-of-the-art office equipment is included.

    The restaurants of Encore feature a variety of cuisines, ranging from premium steaks to classic Italian. The hotel also has 3 on-site nightclubs, including XS Nightclub, which offers a full bar and entertainment by the outdoor pools and cabanas.

    The Las Vegas Encore at Wynn hotel features a Ferrari and Maserati dealership on site. The hotel's fitness center offers daily classes, and personal trainers are available.

    This hotel is within walking distance of all the restaurants and attractions on Las Vegas Boulevard.
